The Sora Family

Word Sora stands for Sky in Japanese, the family abbrviation S being its initial letter. It conveys purity, sanity and idealism.

This color family tends to provide a sky-like feeling, with different variation representing the color of different time of the day.

The Ichigo Family

Word Ichigo stands for Strawberry in Japanese, the family abbrviation I being its initial letter. It conveys relaxation, friendliness and hedonism.

If the Sora family is used as the background, then the Ichigo family plays its role as the tesselating stars.

The different variations represent the different forms of Strawberry, while keeping its characteristics of being sweet and soft.
